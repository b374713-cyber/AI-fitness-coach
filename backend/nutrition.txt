NUTRITION GUIDE

PROTEIN SOURCES: Chicken 31g/100g, Turkey 29g, Beef 26g, Fish 25-30g, Eggs 6g each, Greek yogurt 10g/100g, Cottage cheese 11g/100g, Whey 20-25g/scoop
CARBS: Brown rice 23g/100g, Sweet potato 20g, Oats 66g/100g, Quinoa 21g, Banana 23g each, Berries 15g/cup
FATS: Avocado 15g/100g, Olive oil 14g/tbsp, Nuts 14g/28g, Peanut butter 16g/2tbsp, Fatty fish 13g/100g

WEIGHT LOSS (Cutting): Calorie deficit 300-500 below maintenance, Protein 2.0-2.2g/kg bodyweight, Cardio 3-4x week, HIIT 2x week, 10k steps daily, Sleep 7-9 hours
SAMPLE CUTTING MEAL (2000 cal): Breakfast 3 eggs + 1 cup oats, Lunch 200g chicken + 1 cup rice + broccoli, Dinner 200g fish + sweet potato, Snack Greek yogurt + protein shake

WEIGHT GAIN (Bulking): Calorie surplus 300-500 above maintenance, Protein 1.8-2.0g/kg, Carbs 4-6g/kg, Fats 0.8-1g/kg, Eat 5-6 meals/day, Sleep 8+ hours
LEAN BULK: +200 to +400 kcal surplus, gain 0.25-0.5% bodyweight/week
SAMPLE BULKING MEAL (3000 cal): Breakfast 4 eggs + oats + banana, Lunch 250g chicken + 2 cups rice + avocado, Dinner 250g steak + potato, Snacks protein shake + nuts + Greek yogurt

HIGH CALORIE FOODS: Peanut butter 190 cal/2tbsp, Olive oil 120 cal/tbsp, Nuts 160-200/handful, Whole milk 150/cup, Rice 200/cup, Oats 300/cup, Dates 70 each

PRE-WORKOUT (1-2hr before): Banana + peanut butter + coffee, or Oats + protein shake
POST-WORKOUT (30-45min): Protein shake + banana, then full meal 1hr later

CALORIE CALCULATION (Mifflin-St Jeor):
Men: (10×kg) + (6.25×cm) - (5×age) + 5
Women: (10×kg) + (6.25×cm) - (5×age) - 161
Multiply by activity: Sedentary 1.2, Light 1.375, Moderate 1.55, Very active 1.725, Extra 1.9

MACROS: Protein 1.6-2.2g/kg, Fats 0.8-1g/kg, Carbs = remaining calories ÷ 4
SIMPLE PLATE: 1/2 vegetables, 1/4 protein, 1/4 carbs

MEAL PREP: Prep every 3-4 days, cook proteins in bulk, batch cook rice and potatoes, chop veggies in advance

ESSENTIAL SUPPLEMENTS: Whey protein 20-40g, Creatine 5g daily, Vitamin D3 2000-4000 IU, Omega-3 2-3g, Magnesium 300-400mg before bed
HELPFUL: Caffeine 200-400mg pre-workout, Beta-alanine 3-5g, Citrulline 6-8g pre-workout, Zinc 15-30mg
SAVE MONEY (overhyped): BCAA, Fat burners, Testosterone boosters, Glutamine, CLA, Collagen

HYDRATION: 3-4 liters water daily, bodyweight kg × 0.033 = liters per day, add 500ml per hour of exercise
ELECTROLYTES for workouts over 60min: Sodium 500-1000mg, Potassium (banana, spinach), Magnesium 300-400mg
HOMEMADE ELECTROLYTE DRINK: 1L water + 1/4 tsp salt + lemon juice
SIGNS OF DEHYDRATION: dark yellow urine, dry mouth, headache, fatigue, dizziness, muscle cramps

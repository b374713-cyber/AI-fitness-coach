WORKOUT GUIDE

CHEST DAY: Bench Press 4x8-10, Incline Press 3x10-12, Cable Flyes 3x12-15, Push-ups 3x failure
BACK DAY: Pull-ups 4x8-10, Barbell Rows 3x10-12, Cable Rows 3x12, Deadlifts 3x5-8
LEG DAY: Squats 4x8-10, Leg Press 3x10-12, Lunges 3x12, Leg Extensions 3x12-15, Hamstring Curls 3x12, Calf Raises 4x15-20
SHOULDERS: Overhead Press 4x8-10, Lateral Raises 3x12-15, Front Raises 3x12, Face Pulls 3x15
ARMS: Barbell Curls 3x10-12, Hammer Curls 3x12, Triceps Pushdowns 3x10-12, Skull Crushers 3x10-12, Dips 3x failure

CARDIO: Running 30-45min, Cycling 45-60min, HIIT 20min (30s sprint/30s rest), Jump Rope 15min

SPLITS: 4 day: Chest/Tri, Back/Bi, Legs, Shoulders/Abs. 5 day: Chest, Back, Legs, Shoulders, Arms. PPL: Push, Pull, Legs

PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD: Add weight (2.5-5kg), add reps, add sets, decrease rest, slower tempo, pause reps

INTENSITY TECHNIQUES: Drop sets (reduce weight 20-30% after failure), Supersets (2 exercises no rest), Rest-pause (10-15s rest)

DELOAD: Every 6-8 weeks, reduce weight 40-50%, reduce sets 50%, keep same frequency

SQUAT FORM: Feet shoulder-width, chest up, back straight, go below parallel, drive through heels, knees track over toes
DEADLIFT FORM: Feet hip-width, flat back, chest up, pull slack first, drive legs, keep bar touching legs
BENCH FORM: Feet flat, pinch shoulder blades, arch slightly, lower to sternum, elbows 45-75 degrees
PULL-UP FORM: Grip wider than shoulders, hang fully, pull elbows down, chest to bar, lower controlled

REP RANGES: 1-5 reps = strength, 6-12 reps = muscle growth (best), 12-20 reps = endurance
WEEKLY SETS: Beginner 6-10 sets/muscle, Intermediate 10-16, Advanced 12-20

EXERCISE ORDER: Compounds first (squat, bench, deadlift), then secondary, then isolation last

CARDIO ZONES: Zone 1-2 (50-70% heart rate) fat burning, Zone 3 (70-80%) endurance, HIIT Zone 5
HIIT: 15-20 min, 2-3x per week, 30s sprint/30s rest

FREQUENCY: 1x/week = maintenance, 2x/week = optimal growth, 3x/week = advanced
SRA CYCLE: Stimulus → Recovery (24-48hr) → Adaptation. Train muscle 2x per week for best growth

1RM CALCULATION: Weight × (1 + Reps ÷ 30)
MAX HEART RATE: 220 - age
